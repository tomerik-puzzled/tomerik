ABOUT THE BOOK

The Floor is Full of Sharks is a fun bedtime story about a young boy who creates an elaborate game to avoid his own bedtime.

It comes in a glossy soft cover with 40 pages full of colorful art. It's suitable for kids 2-5 years old.

Created and printed in Toronto.

ABOUT THE AUTHOR

The author and creator is Tom Erik Rossavik. He lives in Toronto, Canada, and far too often has to play elaborate games in order to get his own son to sleep.
